Retail Vs ERetail

The Big Billion day and the market trends!

E-commerce • Oct 17, 2014

'Big Billion Day' offered ERetail created stampede in online space and turnde 'Big Billion Day' in fiasco for the costumers and worry for physical retailers.

Costumers were bothered by unclear policy of limited units of products, site crashed many times during sale due to excess traffic, are such situations which call for regulating the online retail market so that the interest of costumers can be protected and could restore the spoiled reputation of leading ERetailers.

On the other hand big day accentuated worries of physical retailers by offering product below MRP, providing huge discounts. The matter was raised before competition commission of India, but here physical retailers should understand that these are mere platforms or fascilitators who are offering discounts on behalf of producer or wholesellers, and they are able to do so by cutting the long chain of distributors and hence is able to offer huge discounts. Wholeseller is able to do so by passing the discount he got on bulk purchase.

The other important point is number of units carrying discounts were very less. Hence physical retailers cannot call for a legal question of breach of law of competition.

ERetail market needs to be regulated as the future belongs to ERetail.
